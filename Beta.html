  • Historical background

    Marian Boys' Secondary School started with its full operation in 2009.It is under the Holy Ghost Fathers. Marian Boys' SS was built under the guidance and management of the founder,Fr Valentine Bayo CSSp of the Holy Ghost Fathers at Kerege village in Bagamoyo this explains as to why initially was refered to as Kerege Boys' SS.

  • School Mission

    The school is striving to complement, the role of parents, family and community in leading the boys in their care to a great fidelity to God, a sense of justice to all, and love for a neighbor that will form responsible leaders for the future.Always in whatever they do, students are encouraged to strive for excellence, personal integrity, self discipline,respect and tolerance for differences(personal-cultural religion) love for others, a sense of responsibility for the people and the resources of the world, a desire to reach out in a practical way to those in greater need.

  • School Vision

    The school envisions to becoming the best school in Tanzania in NECTA exams and being a source of nobles in this country.

  • Levels Of Education

    The school offers O'level studies with science and business combinations. The school also offers A'level studies with the following combinations.

    1. PCM
    2. PCB
    3. CBG
    4. EGM

  • Number Of STAFF

    The school has both teaching staffs amounting to 40. The school also has non-teaching staffs amounting to 20.
